Visio Lending – Client Order Instructions

 

Integration

  • Platform: Visio has a custom integration built directly with ValueLink.
  • Occasionally, orders such as 1004D or 1007 may be placed outside the integration and direct on our system, which can trigger messages like: “SendOrderComment request failed.” These are expected and can be ignored.

 

Payment & Invoicing

  • Payment Collection: Fastapp will collect payment via payment links.
  • Invoices: Not approved. All orders must be prepaid before assignment or processing.

 

Report Requirements

  • All appraisals must be completed “As-Is.”
  • If the appraiser determines at any point that an As-Is report cannot be provided:
    • The appraiser must STOP work immediately.
    • Notify Fastapp right away for further direction.

 

Appraiser Communication – Condo Questionnaires

If an appraiser requests a condo questionnaire, send this message:

“This client does not order nor provide the condo questionnaire. Please complete the report with all available information and comment on anything unavailable. Attempt to determine if the HOA is in litigation. If unable, please state that accordingly.”

  1. Pause turn time or place the order on hold.
  2. Notify the following team members immediately:
    • @Erica Alba
    • @John Song
    • @Shane Kellum
  3. They will coordinate with @Amanda Loyd to manage the hold and update Visio.

 

Client Communication Protocol

Communication Rules

  • All communication with Visio Account Executives (AEs) regarding appraisal orders, clarifications, or scope of work must occur within the ValueLink portal.
  • Do not respond to appraisal-related emails from AEs.
  • All official communication must be submitted through the portal.

Escalation Rules

  • If an AE contacts you outside the portal, only respond if Don or James are CC’d.
  • If they are not CC’d, forward the message to Don and James and take no further action.

Exceptions – Pricing Requests

  • For pricing questions (bulk orders, 1007s, rural or complex properties):
    Communication via email is acceptable only if Don or James are CC’d.

Follow-Ups & Escalations

  • Continue portal-based follow-ups for one full week if awaiting a client response.
  • If no response after one week, escalate to:
    • Natalie Clutter
    • Ryan Andrews
      They will engage Visio’s management team for updates.

Documentation

Always document protocol-related actions within the order.
Example comment: “Received direct email from AE not following protocol. Forwarded to management per client directive.”

Client Engagement Instructions

Engagement Instructions

IMPORTANT CLIENT ENGAGEMENT INSTRUCTIONS

  • Value must be based on “As-Is” condition. If an “As-Is” report cannot be provided, stop all work and notify the AMC.
  • Health and safety issues: Photograph the issue(s) in full, send photos to the AMC, then stop all work.
  • C6 (Poor) condition rating: Stop. Notify the AMC immediately with photos and do not complete the report.
  • Ineligible property types: If the subject is any of the below, stop and notify the AMC immediately:
    • Vacant Land
    • Mobile Homes
    • Manufactured Homes
    • Log Homes
    • Greater than 4 units
    • Berm Homes
    • Co-ops

 

Valuation Requirements

  • As-Is value only
  • Approaches to value: Use:
    • Sales Comparison Approach
    • Income Approach (when applicable)
    • Cost Approach (required) even if not weighted/not relevant, the client still requires it to be completed.
  • Neighborhood and comp selection: Use special care selecting comps that reflect both physical attributes and the neighborhood setting of the subject.

 

Inspection Requirements

  • Inspection required: Interior and exterior
  • Effective date: Date of inspection

 

Photos and Comparable Photo Requirements

  • All rooms: Lender requires photos of every room (including basement and garage when present).
  • Exterior: Photos of front, back, sides, and street view.
  • Additional structures: Photos of any structures on the premises (including outbuildings).
    • Sheds/outbuildings: Exterior photos only are acceptable.
  • Condition issues: Photograph any interior/exterior deferred maintenance or physical deterioration.
  • Comparable photos (Standards Rule 2-3 requirement): Provide front exterior photos of each comparable sale.

 

Property Characteristics and Eligibility Notes

  • Leasehold: Complete the inspection and notify the AMC if the property is leasehold prior to completing the report.
  • Zoning and use: Zoning must be accurately reported. Lender only lends on residential use. Properties that are not legally zoned or are commercial/mixed use are not acceptable. Highest and Best Use is important and must be addressed clearly.

 

Property Condition (UAD) and Required Actions

  • Report using UAD standards when possible, including a UAD Condition Rating (C1–C6). The Intended User places significant weight on the condition rating and expects it to be accurate.
  • C4 (Avg): Outline any deferred maintenance/repairs needed. Provide cost to cure when applicable.
  • C5 (Fair): Complete the full inspection (all photos and comps). Notify the AMC with photos before completing the report.
  • C6 (Poor): Stop and notify the AMC immediately with photos. Do not complete the report.

 

Transaction, Occupancy, and Rent Reporting

  • Occupancy: Accurately report Owner / Tenant / Vacant.
  • Transaction type: Accurately report Purchase or Refinance.
  • Purchase transactions that include a 1007: The 1007 must include the current rent.

 

Required USPAP / Standards Rule 2-3 Disclosures

  • The report must include Standards Rule 2-3 disclosures, including a prior services statement such as:
    • “I have performed no services, as an appraiser or in any other capacity, regarding the property that is the subject of this report within the three-year period immediately preceding acceptance of this assignment.”
    • OR, if applicable: “I have performed appraisal services regarding the property that is the subject of this report within the three-year period immediately preceding acceptance of this assignment.”

 

Delivery Requirements

  • Deliver both a PDF and XML of the appraisal report.
